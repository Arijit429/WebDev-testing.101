Watch Netflix on your smartphone, tablet, Smart TV, laptop, or streaming device, all for one fixed monthly fee. Plans range from $6.99 to $19.99 a month.

Watch anywhere, anytime. Sign in with your Netflix account to watch instantly on the web or on any internet-connected device.

Netflix is flexible. There are no contracts and no commitments. You can easily cancel your account online in two clicks.
